About Us

Society of Scientists and Engineers (SASE) was founded to help Asian heritage scientifics and engineering professionals achieve their full potential. Our UAH SASE chapter was founded in January 2017 and become nationally recognized in April 2018.


SASE's mission is to

  • Prepare Asian heritage scientists and engineers for success in the global business world
  • Celebrate diversity on campuses and in the workplace
  • Provide opportunities for members to make contributions to their local communities.
